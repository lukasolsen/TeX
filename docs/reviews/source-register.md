## Source acceptance rule

Upstream examples are not copied as policy unless their invariant applies to
TeX. Advice that conflicts with local-first constraints, user-work preservation,
or a stronger platform contract is rejected or narrowed in
`rule-decisions.md`. A source revisit must record whether changed upstream
behavior affects configuration, enforcement, migration, or residual risk.
